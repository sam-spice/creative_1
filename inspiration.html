Inspiration

A dream. Imagine a forest, light pouring through the beautiful boughs of the great, ancient trees surrounding you. Lush, green overgrowth covering the forest floor which itself is teeming with the most wondrous creatures. It was there, pondering on a sturdy rock next to a babbling brook, that an incredible idea formed. This is not that idea. And the rest is history.
